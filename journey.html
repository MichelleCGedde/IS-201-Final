My Journey

My career has not followed one straight line, but each step has taught me something valuable. From teaching to business operations to real estate development, I have learned the importance of communication, organization, adaptability, and continuing to grow.

Teacher | 2001–2010

For nearly a decade, I taught mathematics, science, history, geography, leadership, and personal development at Ivy Hall Academy. Teaching helped me develop strong communication, organization, and leadership skills while discovering the importance of helping others grow and succeed.

Business Development & Executive Support | 2010–2014

My career expanded into executive support, business development, property management, and construction administration. During this time, I learned how organizations operate behind the scenes and gained experience supporting leadership teams, managing projects, coordinating contracts, and building strong professional relationships.

Commercial Real Estate & Construction Administration | 2014–2020

At The Boyer Company, I supported commercial real estate operations and construction projects throughout a large property portfolio. This experience strengthened my skills in project coordination, documentation, reporting, budgeting, and stakeholder communication.

Real Estate Development | 2021–Present

Joining Clyde Capital Group opened the door to large-scale master-planned community development. Today, I support development projects throughout Utah and Idaho, working with project managers, consultants, government agencies, title companies, attorneys, and development partners to help bring communities to life.

My responsibilities include project administration, due diligence coordination, HOA documentation, contract management, title and escrow coordination, and executive support.

Returning to College

One of the most meaningful parts of my journey has been returning to Brigham Young University to complete a goal I began many years ago. While raising a family and building a career, I learned the importance of resilience, adaptability, and perseverance. Those experiences shaped my leadership style, reinforced my belief in continuous growth, and inspired me to pursue higher education later in life.

Balancing work, family, and education has reinforced my belief that learning never ends. Returning to school later in life has challenged me, strengthened my confidence, and reminded me that personal growth has no expiration date.

Looking Ahead

Every step of my journey has prepared me for the next. From the classroom to real estate development, I have learned that success is built through relationships, hard work, adaptability, and a willingness to keep learning.
