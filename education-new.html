Education

Returning to Brigham Young University has been one of the most meaningful chapters of my journey. While building a career, raising a family, and navigating life’s many responsibilities, I never lost sight of my goal to continue my education.

Brigham Young University

Bachelor of General Studies | In Progress

My studies include communications, business, technology, writing, leadership, humanities, and information systems. Each course has helped me build skills that connect directly to my professional work in project management and real estate development.

What I Am Learning

Continuing my education has strengthened my ability to think critically, communicate clearly, solve problems, and adapt to new tools and systems. It has also reminded me that growth often comes from being willing to try something unfamiliar.

Balancing Work, Family & School

Returning to school later in life while working full-time has required discipline, patience, and determination. While raising a family and building a career, I learned the importance of resilience, adaptability, and perseverance. Those experiences shaped my leadership style and strengthened my commitment to continuous growth.

Why It Matters

Education is not simply about earning a degree. It is about continuing to grow, embracing new challenges, and proving to myself that it is never too late to pursue meaningful goals.
