Reinvention at Any Age

I am a Project Management Specialist based in St. George, Utah, with experience in real estate development, project administration, construction documentation, client relations, and operations support.

My career has taken me from teaching to executive support to property management and land development. Along the way, I have learned that growth does not have an expiration date. I am currently continuing my education while working full-time and supporting complex development projects.
